Other Requirements

Cadets at Detachment 730 are held to high standards and expected to exceed expectations. We will help you get to your goals, but as a potenial cadet, you must come prepared in order succeed in our program.
ACADEMICS

Once in the program, cadets must pass LLAB and AS class with at least a C-. Scholarship cadets must maintain a GPA above 2.5.

FITNESS

Cadets must meet weight and fitness standards, including passing the Physical Fitness Assessment every semester, in order to obtain and remain on scholarship.

MEDICAL

Cadets must be medically eligibile to enter active duty. Visit the Air Force ROTC webite in order to learn more about the medical requirements.

ATTENDANCE

While cadets must attend 80% of both LLAB and PT, 100% attendance is the standard. Cadets are also expected to be actively involved in other AFROTC events.
