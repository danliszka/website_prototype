Our Program
What is Air Force ROTC?

AFROTC is a commissioning program into the United States Air Force. While completing a standard college education, we also learn the skills and qualities necessary to become an Air Force Officer. Upon graduation and completion of training, cadets commission as second lieutenants in the world's greatest Air Force.
Program Requirements

AS Class

Aerospace studies is a weekly academic course taught by cadre. It focuses on Air Force history, leadership theory, and life on Active Duty.

LLAB

Leadership Laboratory is conducted on Thursday mornings every week. During these 2 hours, cadets put their leadership into practice by working together to tackle challenging tasks and learn new skills.

PT

Weekly physical training sessions help keep cadets fit and build morale. Cadets are expected to maintain a high level of fitness in order to be prepared to enter the Air Force upon commissioning.
