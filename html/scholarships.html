Scholarships
Interested in Using Air Force ROTC to Help Pay For College?

AFROTC offers a variety of scholarships. There are multiple ways to earn them, depending on your current status. See below for the path that applies to you.

For High School Students

High school seniors apply for this scholarship on their own through the AFROTC website. You must apply by December 1st. There are full and partial scholarships offered under this option.

For College Students

In college scholarships are awarded based on performance once you have joined AFROTC. Scholarships are available to students with at least 6 semesters remaining in your collge program.

For Enlisted Airmen

There are several programs through which elisted members can earn commission through Air Force ROTC at more than 1,100 schools. These programs and their requirements are frequently changing.

Types of Scholarships

Type 1

Pays full tuition and most fees.
Offered to high school students.

Type 2

Pays tuition and most fees up to $18,000 per year.
Offered to high school and college students.

Type 7

Pays full tuition and fees for in-state schools.
Offered to high school and college students.
