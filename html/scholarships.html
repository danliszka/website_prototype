Scholarships
Interested in Using Air Force ROTC to Help Pay For College?

There are two ways to earn scholarships and three different types of scholarships to be awarded. Check your eligibility here.

For High School Students

High school seniors apply for this scholarship on their own. You must apply by December 1st. There are full and partial scholarships offered under this option. For more information about the application process click here.

For College Students

In college scholarships are awarded based on performance once you have joined AFROTC. Scholarships are available to students with at least 6 semesters remaining in your collge program. Talk to cadre to learn more.
Types of Scholarships


Type 1

Pays full tuition and most fees.
Offered to high school students.

Type 2

Pays tuition and most fees up to $18,000 per year.
Offered to high school and college students.

Type 7

Pays full tuition and fees for in-state schools.
Offered to high school and college students.
