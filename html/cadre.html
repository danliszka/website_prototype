Captain Michael Gorrell is the Assistant Professor of Aerospace Studies and Recruiting Flight Commander, Air Force Reserve Officer Training Corps (ROTC) Detachment 730, University of Pittsburgh. He is responsible for recruiting, training, and educating Air Force officer candidates in preparation for active duty.

Capt Gorrell entered the Air Force in 2008 as a graduate of the Pennsylvania State University Air Force ROTC program. His assignments include tours in program management, test, and operations for missile defense and space systems. In 2015 he deployed overseas in support of Operation Inherent Resolve.

Prior to his arrival at Detachment 730, Capt Gorrell was the Lead, Advanced Extremely High Frequency (AEHF) Flight Operations at the Space and Missiles Systems Center, Los Angeles Air Force Base, California. He led a team of more than 100 personnel in the launch, early orbit, and on-orbit operations of space systems providing secure, survivable, and jam-resistant communications for the National Command Authority and Combatant Commanders.
